# Ko-fi Setup for QQ (施清荃)

Since GitHub Sponsors, Patreon, Stripe, and Open Collective don't support China, we'll use **Ko-fi + PayPal** as the alternative.

## Why Ko-fi?

- ✅ Supports PayPal (which works in China)
- ✅ No platform fees for donations
- ✅ Simple one-time or monthly support
- ✅ Works internationally
- ✅ Can withdraw to PayPal, then to Chinese bank

---

## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Create PayPal Account (if not already)

1. Go to https://www.paypal.com/c2/home
2. Sign up with your email
3. Verify your Chinese bank account or card
4. Complete identity verification

**Note:** PayPal China has some limitations but works for receiving international payments.

### Step 2: Create Ko-fi Account

1. Go to https://ko-fi.com
2. Click "Start a Page"
3. Sign up with your email or Google account
4. Choose username: **qqshi13** or **qqtools** (something memorable)

### Step 3: Set Up Your Ko-fi Page

**Page Settings:**
- **Page Name:** QQ's Developer Tools
- **Tagline:** "Building free web tools for developers"
- **About:** 
  ```

  I build free, open-source web tools to help developers work more efficiently.
  
  My projects include:
  - ⏰ Time & Flow - Pomodoro timer
  ```

### Step 4: Configure Payment

1. Go to Ko-fi Dashboard → Payment Settings
2. Connect PayPal account
3. Set minimum donation: **¥5 (or $1)**
4. Enable monthly subscriptions (optional)

### Step 6: Get Your Ko-fi Link

## Withdrawing Money from Ko-fi

### Ko-fi → PayPal
1. Log into Ko-fi Dashboard
2. Go to "Balance" or "Payments"
3. Click "Withdraw to PayPal"
4. Money arrives in PayPal in 1-3 days

### PayPal → Chinese Bank
1. Log into PayPal China
2. Go to "Wallet" → "Withdraw"
3. Add your Chinese bank account
4. Withdraw RMB to your bank
5. Usually takes 3-5 business days

**Note:** PayPal China has a withdrawal fee (around 1-2%)
