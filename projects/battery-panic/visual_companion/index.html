Battery Panic - Pixel Art Style Options

A. 8-bit NES Style
Characteristics: Limited 54-color palette, hard edges, iconic retro look
  • Authentic nostalgic feel
  • Small file sizes
  • Less detail for complex sprites
  • Can look dated
B. 16-bit SNES Style
Characteristics: 32,768 colors, gradients, more detail, Mode 7 effects possible
  • Rich, detailed graphics
  • Still clearly pixel art
  • More work to create
  • Larger sprites needed
C. Modern Minimalist
Characteristics: Flat colors, simple shapes, clean aesthetic, contemporary
  • Quick to produce
  • Scales well to all sizes
  • Modern appeal
  • Less "gamey" feeling
D. Game Boy Style
Characteristics: 4-shade monochrome green, extreme constraints, unique aesthetic
  • Very distinctive look
  • Extremely fast to create
  • Limited expressiveness
  • Niche appeal
