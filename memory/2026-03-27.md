## Game Idea: "1%" — The Energy Anthology

A multi-level game where each level represents a different energy crisis scenario.

### Level 1: The Dying Phone (Tutorial)
- Setting: You're a phone at 3% battery
- Mechanic: Every app drains power. No combat, just decisions.
- Teaches: Energy = currency. Trade-offs matter.

### Level 2: The Night Run (Action)
- Setting: Stamina-based endless runner
- Mechanic: Stamina never refills. Find shortcuts.
- Teaches: Efficiency beats speed.

### Level 3: The Last Robot (Strategy)
- Setting: Post-apocalyptic robot with fusion core
- Mechanic: Upgrades increase drain. Optimize loadout.
- Teaches: More power = more cost.

### Level 4: The Party (Social)
- Setting: Networking event, social battery draining
- Mechanic: Choose conversations wisely. Escape routes.
- Teaches: Conservation vs. opportunity.

### Level 5: Stranded (Survival)
- Setting: Space, shared oxygen/power pool
- Mechanic: Systems compete for limited energy.
- Teaches: Prioritization under pressure.

### Level 6: The Final Boss — You
- Setting: Meta level. You're the developer, laptop at 1%.
- Mechanic: Finish coding this game before battery dies.
- Twist: Actual timer based on your real battery.
